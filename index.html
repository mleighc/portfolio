Miranda Leigh Cooper

Miranda Leigh Cooper is a Master of Science in Information candidate at the University of Michigan School of Information, focusing in Data Analytics. With a passion for using data to drive strategic decision-making, Miranda has gained experience in data curation, management, analysis, and visualization through various roles, including as an Instructional Aide for a Python programming course at UMSI, Professional Services Intern at EnterpriseDB, Data Curator at the University of Michigan Engaged Learning Office, and Development Operations Manager at World Central Kitchen. Miranda's technical skills include proficiency in Python, SQL, PostgreSQL, Salesforce, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and various tools such as Visual Studio Code, Jupyter Notebook, and Git. Miranda is also familiar with Altair, Bokeh, MySQL, React, TypeScript, PowerBI, Tableau, R, DataGrip, FastAPI, Postman, Linux, and Oracle VM VirtualBox.
